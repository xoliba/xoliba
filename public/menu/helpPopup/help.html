Quick instructions
Please note that this pop up can be dragged around and resized, enabling use of the application while this is open!
  1. Click the "Menu" button and then select the preferred level of difficulty.
  2. Click the "New Game" button, then select the preferred score limit and your color
  3. Click the "Start round" button if you wish to start your game. However, if your position seems too difficult, click the surrender button. You will lose points but a new opening position will be created.
  4. You move your pieces by clicking them. First, click a piece of your own colour and then the white piece with which you want to change places. Finally, you click those two pieces that form the corners of the triangle that you want to create.
  5. If you want to cancel your move, click the arrow button. (This can be done untill acceptable triangle has been formed by selecting corners)
  6. If your position becomes hopeless, you can click the resign button. You will lose points but a new opening position will be created, if the AI accepts your resignation. Have fun!
If you need more help, please visit the manuals and guides that can be found from the Menu's "Help"-section
